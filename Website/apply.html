How to Apply

If you would like to participate on the course then please register your interest by completing the application form below. Experience running this course in previous years has shown that there is a high demand for this course and so rather than simply taking applications on a first-come first-served basis, we try to select participants who will benefit most from this course. It is therefore important that you clearly state your area of research and your motivation for joining the course on your application. Applications will be reviewed at midday on 30th June 2017 and the successful candidates will be notified within two weeks of this date. Upon notification, the successful candidates will be asked to verify their attendance and pay the course costs. The total cost for the course is 5600 Norwegian kronor (approximately 590€ or £500). This fee includes the following:

  • Course study materials and tuition
  • 5 nights accommodation at the Lygra Gjestegard from 24th September to 29th September
  • All meals during the stay at Lygra: an evening meal on the night of the 24th September, and breakfast, lunch, and dinner on the full teaching days (25th - 29th September)
  • Tea, coffee, and snacks

The payment of the course fees will be due by the 20th July 2017. After this date, unpaid course places may be re-allocated to waiting candidates. After the first selection process, any remaining places will be allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.

Feel free to direct any questions related to this course to Joe Chipperfield. Please note that, if you are unable to attend the course, we cannot guarantee a full refund of the course fees if we are not informed before 20th July 2017.

Your application will be confirmed with an email. Sometimes this email can take a day or two to arrive. If you do not receive a confirmation email then we will not have received your application. In this instance, please contact Joe Chipperfield with your application details and we will ensure that your application is registered.
